Work Flow Action (WFA)

 

 

 

automated task with a set duration that an Agent assigns to a Candidate to aid in the processing of Candidates and Clients
WFA1: Talk to the Candidate via a phone conversation and then send a confirmation email outlining that their submission details have been successfully received and are now in the process of matching the Candidate to a placement. 
WFA2:Talk to the candidate, the candidate has not meet the client skills for placement, but the consultant keep the contact with the candidate
WFA3: Talk to the candidate to check if he/she has a currently a job and propose a new job to the candidate.
